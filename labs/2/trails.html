The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
  1. Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet1 off the ground.
  2. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor2, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
  1. Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove3.
  2. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
  • Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with light layers of clothing.
  • It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
WashRoom Facilities

All the guests will get the opportunity to use our luxury whirlpool® and Pool® inside our luxury resort area.
