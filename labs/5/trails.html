The Shephard Luxury Resort Trail is growing rapidly, with 875k annual visits, and is your go-to website for trail professionals, volunteers, stewards, and the industry that they support. We work on behalf of all trail types, motorized and non motorized, hiking, biking, equestrian, water, and snow trails. Our mission is to advance the development of diverse, high quality trails and greenways. Through collaboration, education, and communication, we raise awareness of the value of trails
